DEFINATION LIST

  • what is the super computer

A supercomputer is a computer with a high level of performance as compared to a general-purpose computer.

  • super computer vs normal computer

Supercomputers have very high storage capacity and can store thousands of times more data than an average personal computer. The supercomputer can process billions of instructions in a second. Modern supercomputers have thousands of processors and are used for complex computations.
